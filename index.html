About Jim's Enterprises

Jim's Enterprises has been in the PPR & PVC industry for more than 20 years.

Jim's Enterprises envisions a community of growth and heart, a community that strives for advancement. We are able to serve this purpose on the basis of industrialization by supplying the preeminent pipe system product line in the Philippines. We provide high quality pipes to developers, contractors, architects, engineers, retailers and such consumers at sensible costs. Jim's Enterprises has thrived for more than twenty years to equip its clients only with the best.

Today, we stand as the most competitive supplier of PPR and PVC water piping system products in the Philippines. Our materials range on a global scale, bringing the highest quality of the most elite of brands to the Philippines.
